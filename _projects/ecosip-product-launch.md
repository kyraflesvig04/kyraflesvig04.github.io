## Overview  
Developed a comprehensive product launch strategy for EcoSip, a smart reusable water bottle that blends hydration technology with sustainability. The campaign positioned EcoSip as a wellness-driven innovation targeting health-conscious and eco-conscious consumers.

## Agency Concept  
Modern Springs Media – a sustainability-focused brand innovation studio specializing in behavior-driven ecosystems and intelligent product branding.

## Objectives  
- Achieve 40% brand awareness among ages 18–30  
- Secure 4+ influencer partnerships in wellness & sustainability  
- Generate 15,000+ social followers within 3–4 months  
- Maintain 85% positive product review sentiment  

## Target Publics  
**Consumers:**  
- Athletes  
- Students  
- Busy professionals  
- Health & environmentally conscious individuals  

**Organizations:**  
- Fitness centers  
- Universities  
- Athletic brands  
- Health-focused retailers  

## Key Message  
EcoSip isn’t just a smart bottle — it upgrades daily hydration habits while reducing single-use plastic waste.

**Tagline:**  
Hydrate Smarter. Live Sustainably.

## Strategy  
- Influencer partnerships  
- Social-first launch on Instagram & TikTok  
- University & gym collaborations  
- Student discounts and giveaways  
- Press release distribution  

## PR Component  
Drafted a full press release announcing product launch, highlighting:  
- UV-C self-cleaning technology  
- Real-time hydration tracking  
- Sustainable stainless-steel design  
- 24-hour cold / 12-hour hot insulation  
- $79.99 launch pricing

Sustainability Marketing

Integrated Communications

## Overview
Developed a comprehensive marketing launch strategy for WHOOP Stride, a self-cleaning, charge-free smart sock designed to deliver real-time foot-based biometric performance data.

This group project focused on expanding WHOOP’s product ecosystem into smart apparel while maintaining its premium performance positioning.

## My Role
- Conducted market research and competitive analysis  
- Developed full SWOT analysis  
- Built segmentation, targeting, and positioning strategy  
- Designed pricing model and subscription integration  
- Created multi-channel promotional strategy  
- Established KPIs and financial projections  

## The Challenge
WHOOP needed to enter a new smart apparel category while differentiating from major wearable competitors such as Apple, Garmin, and Oura. The strategy required balancing innovation, affordability, and recurring subscription revenue.

## The Strategy
- Penetration pricing model to drive rapid adoption  
- Direct-to-consumer first distribution strategy  
- Influencer and marathon-city activation campaigns  
- Subscription-based performance analytics integration  
- Strategic retail partnerships (Dick’s Sporting Goods, Fleet Feet)  

## Key Metrics & Goals
- 25% projected sales growth within 12 months  
- 10% subscription growth target  
- $750,000 structured launch budget  
- 30% increase in social engagement

## Organization
Lewis University Athletics  

## Role
Athletic Communications Intern  

## Overview
Supported athletic communications operations across multiple varsity programs, focusing on digital storytelling, brand consistency, and game-day media execution.

## Responsibilities
- Designed branded social media graphics for game days and announcements  
- Managed live stats and wrote post-game recaps  
- Assisted with press releases and website updates  
- Supported in-game media operations  

## Impact
- Increased engagement through consistent visual branding  
- Delivered timely, professional game coverage  
- Strengthened the athletic department’s digital presence  

## Skills Used
Sports marketing, digital media strategy, content creation, brand communication, event operations
